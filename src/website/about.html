💻 What I Do

I write code. A lot of it. Some of it actually works. My primary language of choice is Lua with LOVE2D, because simplicity is key… until I decide to overcomplicate things with my own libraries.

🎯 My Philosophy

I strongly believe that:

  • **Software should be lightweight.** If your app takes 2GB of RAM to say "Hello, World," delete it.
  • **Frameworks are traps.** If you can't do it in raw code, do you *really* know what you're doing?
  • **Error messages should be entertaining.** If my program crashes, at least you'll get a good laugh.
  • **Simplicity > Bloat.** If my code runs faster than yours, I win.
